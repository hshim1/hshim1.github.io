I'm a bioinformatician at the University of Chicago. My recent work
has been in bulk RNA-seq and multi-omics analysis, studying
Hedgehog signaling-dependent gene regulatory networks in
cardiogenesis.

Before moving into bioinformatics, I spent seven years doing
translational bench research across neurobiology, immunology, and
vascular biology. I work in R and Python and build reproducible
pipelines with Nextflow and Snakemake.

## Research focus

- RNA-seq (bulk, single-cell, single-nucleus), ATAC-seq, ChIP-seq,
  and multi-omics integration
- Reproducible workflows with Nextflow, Snakemake, and SLURM
- NGS platforms: 10x Genomics, Illumina, Oxford Nanopore
